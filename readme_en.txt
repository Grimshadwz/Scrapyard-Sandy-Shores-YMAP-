**Content**
Over 400 items !!!!
The map is suitable for RP situations, it can be used as a towing service / mechanic with TÜV test center!
Here is a small overview ..


* Indoor *
1x sales room,
1x office space,
1x workshop,
2x lifts,
2x office tables,
1x sales table,
Various items (computers, monitors, spoilers, decos, etc.),
3x door,
2x goal (Benny),
lamps

* Exterior *
1x stage with driveway for underbody inspection;) (RP TÜV),
1x lift,
3x lift with cars (WARNING !!!! Cars explode when you climb the ladder),
2x gates,
Lamps / headlights,
Various objects (tool boxes, jacks, scrap etc.)

* Junkyard *
Junkyard with various scrap cars,
Fire barrel with fire,
illegal shooting range,
Heli,
Destroyed plane,
Various fences,
1x goal
